Fractals of the Mind

Project Overview

The Challenge

Represent abstract emotions and internal states through photography rather than traditional visuals.

The Solution

Use long exposure techniques to capture movement and light as expressive elements, distorting reality into emotional compositions.

The Outcome

A visual series that explores perception, time, and emotion through experimental photography.
